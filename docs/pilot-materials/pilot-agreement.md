### 1. Purpose

This informal agreement outlines the terms of a 90-day pilot partnership to evaluate Benefits Navigator's platform for [VSO case management / veteran claims assistance].

---

### 2. What Benefits Navigator Provides

During the pilot period, BN will provide Partner with:

- [ ] **Full platform access** — All features, no limitations
- [ ] **User accounts** — Up to _____ seats (caseworkers/staff)
- [ ] **Onboarding support** — Initial training session (30-60 min)
- [ ] **Priority support** — Direct access to the BN team via email/Slack
- [ ] **No cost** — The pilot is completely free

---

### 3. What Partner Agrees To

In exchange for free pilot access, Partner agrees to:

- [ ] **Minimum usage** — At least 3 active users engaging with the platform
- [ ] **Weekly check-ins** — 15-minute call or async update each week
- [ ] **End-of-pilot interview** — 30-minute feedback session at pilot conclusion
- [ ] **Honest feedback** — Share what's working and what isn't
- [ ] **Permission to use metrics** — BN may use anonymized usage data (no PII) to improve the product

---

### 4. Data & Privacy

- Partner data remains Partner's property
- BN will not share Partner's data with third parties (except as required for platform operation, e.g., OpenAI for AI features)
- BN's privacy policy applies: [URL]
- Partner can request data deletion at any time

---

### 5. What Happens After the Pilot

At the end of the 90-day pilot:

**Option A: Continue as Paying Customer**
- Transition to paid plan at mutually agreed pricing
- Data and accounts carry over seamlessly
- Pilot feedback informs any custom pricing

**Option B: End Partnership**
- Partner's data exported or deleted (Partner's choice)
- No obligation, no hard feelings
- BN may follow up periodically about future interest

---

### 6. No Legal Obligations

This is an informal partnership agreement, not a binding contract. Either party may end the pilot at any time for any reason.
