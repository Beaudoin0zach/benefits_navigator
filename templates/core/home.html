Why Most Veterans Get Underrated

  • Pushing through pain during ROM tests (military training works against you)
  • Minimizing symptoms ("It's not that bad" kills your claim)
  • Not mentioning flare-ups and worst days
  • Not knowing the DBQ form the examiner uses
  • Confusing VA terminology (nexus, secondary, aggravation)
  • Missing appeal deadlines or choosing wrong appeal lane
