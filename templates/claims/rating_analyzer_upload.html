What to Expect

Analysis typically takes 1-2 minutes. You'll get:

  • A summary of your current ratings with diagnostic codes
  • Specific opportunities to increase each rating
  • Secondary conditions you should consider claiming
  • Potential errors in your rating decision
  • Appeal deadline tracking
  • Benefits you're eligible for at your current rating
  • C&P exam preparation guidance

Tips for Best Results

  • Upload the full rating decision letter (all pages)
  • Make sure the document is readable (not blurry or faded)
  • PDF format works best for multi-page documents
  • If you have multiple decisions, upload the most recent one first

Your documents are encrypted and stored securely. We use them only to provide analysis and will never share your personal information.
