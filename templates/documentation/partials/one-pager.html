Free tools to help veterans understand, file, and win VA disability claims

100% Free Core Features Built by Veterans, for Veterans WCAG AA Accessible

The Problem

  • • Over 30% of VA claims are denied on first submission
  • • Veterans wait 125+ days on average for claim decisions
  • • Many veterans don't know what benefits they qualify for
  • • VA letters and regulations are confusing and bureaucratic
  • • C&P exams are often the make-or-break moment, but veterans go in unprepared

Our Solution

VA Benefits Navigator provides free, accessible tools that guide veterans through every step of the claims process - from understanding what conditions qualify, to preparing for C&P exams, to appealing denials.

Core Features

VA Rating Calculator

Calculate your combined VA disability rating using official VA Math (38 CFR § 4.25). Includes bilateral factor, SMC eligibility, TDIU checker, and 2024 compensation rates.

C&P Exam Guides

Condition-specific guides for PTSD, TBI, Musculoskeletal, Hearing, Sleep Apnea, and Mental Health exams. Know what to expect, what to say, and what not to do.

Denial Decoder

Upload your VA denial letter and get plain-English explanations of why you were denied, relevant M21 manual sections, and specific guidance on what evidence to gather for appeal.

Secondary Conditions Hub

Discover 40+ documented secondary condition relationships with medical rationale, evidence tips, and nexus letter guidance. PTSD, TBI, back, knee, diabetes, and more.

Appeals Workflow

Interactive decision tree to choose the right appeal lane (HLR, Supplemental, BVA). Step-by-step guides, deadline tracking, and document management.

VA Glossary

86 VA terms explained in plain English. Search or browse definitions for DBQ, nexus, BVA, TDIU, SMC, and all the acronyms veterans encounter.
