AI Document Processing

Benefits Navigator uses AI technology to analyze your VA documents and provide helpful insights. When you upload documents with AI analysis enabled, your document text is processed using OpenAI's API services.

How We Use AI:

  • Document Analysis: Extract key information from rating decisions and denial letters
  • Increase Opportunities: Identify potential rating increases based on VA criteria
  • Appeal Guidance: Decode denial reasons and suggest evidence for appeals

Important Privacy Information:

  • Document text is sent to OpenAI's API for processing
  • Per OpenAI's data usage policy, API inputs may be used to improve their models unless you opt out
  • We use OpenAI's API with data retention disabled for enhanced privacy
  • We never share your personal information or documents with third parties for marketing
